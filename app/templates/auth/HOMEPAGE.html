Latest insights

Interpret the signals that matter most in directed evolution.

This portal is designed to make core experimental signals easier to read, compare, and explain. From DNA and protein yield to activity-normalised enzyme efficiency, each run turns raw measurements into interpretable evidence for selection decisions.

DNA Yield

Monitor recovery and amplification performance.

DNA yield highlights how strongly each construct is recovered after the assay, making it easier to spot variants that persist, amplify cleanly, or drop off across generations.

Read plasmid purification yield paper

Protein Yield

Separate expression strength from apparent activity.

Protein yield and expression context show whether a variant looks strong because it is highly expressed, or because it is genuinely producing better catalytic output.

Read protein expression tuning paper

Enzyme Efficiency

Use activity score to compare functional efficiency.

The activity score combines normalised DNA and protein measurements so high-ranking variants reflect stronger enzyme efficiency, not just higher abundance.

Read enzyme kinetics review

Directed Evolution

Follow selection pressure through generations.

By comparing lineage, distributions, and mutation structure together, the portal makes it easier to identify adaptive branches and explain why certain variants continue to dominate.

Read PubMed evolution review
